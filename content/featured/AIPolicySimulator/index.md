Developed an innovative simulation tool leveraging Agentic AI for the Ministry of Law and Human Rights (Kemenkumham). The system enables government officials to simulate, analyze, and predict the outcomes of implementing new legal policies before they are officially enacted within the AHU Online ecosystem. Built with a Decision Support System utilizing autonomous AI agents to automate complex workflows and provide actionable, data-driven policy insights.
